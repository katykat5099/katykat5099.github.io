Mystie Forest

Monsters

Gekkou

Gekkou is your starter, known as your "Partner" by folk in the game.
It's species is bred specifically by the people of the Forest, but has always been considered extinct in the wild.
Outsiders don't even know of it's existance.

Mystie Forest researchers of the Village are given Gekkou to train and once it's fully grown, is proof the researcher is ready to work. The better trained a Gekkou is, is said to mean the specific researcher is naturally gifted.
