Vantaro

Vantaro is a spirit that lives within the ruins. It's a pretty shy spirit, but its potential to be very dangerous is quite large. It specializes in magical abilities, best way to beat it is to not fight it at all. Due to its shy nature, simply do not look at it and you will not need to worry about an encounter with it.
