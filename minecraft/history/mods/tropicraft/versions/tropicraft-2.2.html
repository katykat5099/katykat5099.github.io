New things since last version:

Mobs

This version adds 4 new types of frogs, and a "head" mob.

Iguanas are naturally hostile toward Frogs.

Mobs in this Version:

Iguana

Drop 1-2 Scales

Appears to spawn in warm Forests.

Frog

Drop Frog Legs

Poisonous Frogs

Are hostile toward the player, and shoot poison darts.
Some are really fast, and some are slow but float like chickens.

They drop Poison frog skin

There are 3 variants.

Red

Yellow

Blue

Head

Primarily Passive mob, will attack if touched in any way.

Passive stage (far left). Not aggressive.
Warning stage (middle). Not aggressive.
Angry stage (far right). Aggressive and attacking.

Features in this Version:

World Generation

Adds several plants to generate in the world naturally.

Pineapple is a 2 block high plant that randomly generates on top of grass in warm biomes.

The Orchid is a plant that randomly generates on top of grass in warm biomes.

The Iris is a 2 block high plant that randomly generates on top of grass in warm biomes.

The Crocosmia is a plant that randomly generates on top of grass in warm biomes.

The Commelina Diffusa is a plant that randomly generates on top of grass in warm biomes.

The Palm Tree is a tree that randomly generated on top of sand in warm biomes. It has two variants.

A Bamboo plant is a cane-type plant that randomly generates in patches on top of Grass in warm biomes. It drops Bamboo when destroyed.
