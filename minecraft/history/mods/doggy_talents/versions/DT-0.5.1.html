Mobs in this Version:

Wolves

Tamed Wolves can be upgraded by teaching them new talents. There are several levels for a specific talent, and the chance of a successful upgrade is lowered each time it gains a new level. Upgrading uses an available Training Treat in the inventory.

Right-clicking a Tamed Wolf with a Stick comes up with a new GUI that displays the Wolf's mastery level and skill levels, and the Player can also use this menu to rename their Wolf.

Tamed Wolves can now be commanded to Wander by right-clicking them with a Bone. They will wander around the designated zone until commanded to Sit.

Wolves that reach 0 HP do not permanently die, rather loose their perks and become weak. All can be recovered by feeding them a Cake.

Wolves can be released back into the wild using Shears while at full health. It is a 1/64 chance to work, but takes no durability to do so. They keep their old talents.

Skills for Wolves

BlackPelt

A Wolf skill using Leather. Increases the Wolf's attack power on successful level up.

GuardDog

A Wolf skill using an Iron Ingot. Increases the Wolf's defense on successful level up.

HellHound

A Wolf skill using Netherrack. Increases the Wolf's immunity to Fire and Lava on successful level up.

PillowPaw

A Wolf skill using a Feather. Increases the Wolf's immunity to fall damage on successful level up.

Creeper Sweeper

A Wolf skill using Gunpowder. Wolves will growl at Creepers to warn you.

DogLight

A Wolf skill using a Glowstone Block. Wolves will produce more Torches when overfed per level.

WolfMount

A Wolf skill using a Saddle. Wolves are ride-able, and run faster per level. Can be mounted by right-clicking the Wolf while in the air.

If the Wolf also has the DoggyDash skill, the WolfMount skill's movement is improved.*

If the Wolf also has the FisherDog skill, the WolfMount skill now has the ability to swim. Requires FisherDog level 5.

PackPuppy

A Wolf skill using a Chest. The Wolf will now have an Inventory to store items in. The Inventory can be accessed by ctrl-right-clicking the Wolf.

DoggyDash

A Wolf skill using a Gold Ingot. The Wolf will now lunge at enemies.*

FisherDog

A Wolf skill using String. The Wolf can now "fish" for Fish while in water. They will bring back the Fish after shaking off their pelts.
