Doggy Talents v1.5.8

New things since last version:

Leveling system has been changes, instead of having a random chance to gain a level based on the total level count, it now has skill points to use to upgrade skills.
Every time you feed the Dog a Training Treat, it will level up and gain more skill points. Eventually the Training Treat will no longer be enough to level up, and you must upgrade to Super Treats... then Master Treats.

Mobs in this Version:

Wolves

Wolves can either be tamed into a Tamed Wolf with bones, or tamed into a new entity called a Doggy Talents Dog with a Training Treat.

Tamed Wolves can be turned into a Doggy Talents Dog with a Training Treat.

Tamed Wolves are unaffected by any other Doggy Talents mechanics.

Doggy Talents Dog (Dog)

States

Dogs have 5 custom states besides Following and Sitting.
You can view their State from a letter above their name.

  • Wander - Dogs can be commanded to Wander in around the area they were designated to wander in using an Arrow. They will not follow you, and it can be cancelled any time by commanding them to Sit.

  • Docile - Dogs in the Docile state will not attack any mobs ever. You must change their tactic for them to fight for you. Their combat state can be changed with a Sign.

  • Tactical - Dogs in the Tactical state will only attack mobs on command. You can use the Command emblem to throw a harmless projectile at a nearby target, and the Dog will respond on hit. Their combat state can be changed with a Sign.

  • Aggressive - Dogs in the Aggressive state will attack mobs that the player attacks, or mobs that attack the player. The same as Tamed Wolves. Their combat state can be changed with a Sign.

  • Berserk - Dogs in the Berserk state will attack any mob that is hostile to the player, regardless of if the player is in any danger. Their combat state can be changed with a Sign.

    • Level System

    Stats Screen

    Right-clicking a Dog with a Stick comes up with a new GUI that displays the Dog's skill levels, mastery level, and the Player can also use this menu to name their Dog.

    Levels

    Dogs have a skill tree and leveling system. Each level gives the Dog more skill points to be used. From levels 1-34, use Training Treats to level up. From levels 35-67, use Super Treats, and 68-100 use Master Treats.

    Skill Points

    Dogs can be upgraded by teaching them new talents. There are 5 levels for each kind of talent. Each time a talent is upgraded/leveled-up, it uses some of the Dog's available skill points. The higher level the Dog is, the more skill points it needs to level up a talent. Talents can be trained by right-clicking the Dog with the correct training item. You can view a list of items in the Dog's stats screen.

    Misc

    Dogs naturally regenerate health based on a new stat called "nourishment". It can be replenished with meat, and 3 - 6 meat is needed to refill from zero depending on the type of meat used. The nourishment limit is 120, and higher nourishment heals quicker. Nourishment levels can be seen in brackets next to their custom name.

    Dogs have 16 different alternate skins to choose from. You can switch the selected skin using a Lapis Lazuli Block. Each click rotates through the range of colors.

    Dogs that reach 0 HP do not permanently die, rather loose their perks and become weak. All can be recovered by feeding them a Cake.

    Dogs can be released back into the wild using Collar Shears while at full health. It is a 1/64 chance to work, but takes no durability to do so. They keep their old talents.

    Skills for Dogs

    BlackPelt

    A Dog skill using Leather. Increases the Dog's attack power on successful level up. At Level 5, attack power's nearly comparable to a Diamond Sword.

    GuardDog

    A Dog skill using an Iron Ingot. Increases the Dog's defense on successful level up. At Level 5, the dog would recieve only half the damage being dealt.

    HellHound

    A Dog skill using Netherrack. Causes the Dog's attacks to light their enemies on fire. at Level 5, they gain immunity to Fire and Lava.

    HunterDog

    A Dog skill using Flint. Causes Mobs killed by the Dog to drop twice, with separate drop rates.

    PillowPaw

    A Dog skill using a Feather. Increases the Dog's immunity to fall damage on successful level up.

    Creeper Sweeper

    A Dog skill using Gunpowder. Wolves will growl at Creepers to warn you.

    DogLight

    A Dog skill using a Glowstone Block. Wolves will produce more Torches when overfed per level.

    WolfMount

    A Dog skill using a Saddle. Wolves are ride-able, and run faster per level. Can be mounted by right-clicking the Dog while in the air.

    If the Dog also has the DoggyDash skill, the WolfMount skill's movement is improved.

    If the Dog also has the FisherDog skill level 5, the WolfMount skill now has the ability to swim.

    If the Dog also has the PillowPaw skill, the WolfMount skill will have an improved jump height.

    If the Dog also has the HellHound skill level 5, the WolfMount skill will protect you from fire and lava.

    PackPuppy

    A Dog skill using a Chest. The Dog will now have an Inventory to store items in. The Inventory can be accessed by ctrl-right-clicking the Dog. The inventory gains 3 slots per level, with a maximum of 15 levels.

    DoggyDash

    A Dog skill using a Gold Ingot. The Dog will move faster toward their enemies when attacking, preventing mobs from running away or jumping.

    FisherDog

    A Dog skill using String. The Dog can now "fish" for Fish while in water. They will drop Fish after shaking off their pelts. At Level 5, they can breathe underwater.

    HappyEater

    A Dog skill using a Wooden Bowl. Increases the Dog's nourishment intake by 10% per level. At level 3, allows them to eat Fish. At level 5, allows them to eat Rotten Flesh.

    Skins

    Dogs have 16 extra available skins to choose from. You can cycle through them by right clicking on the Dog while holding a Lapis Lazuli Block. Doing this does not use the block.
