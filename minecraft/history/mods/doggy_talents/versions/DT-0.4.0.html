Doggy Talents v0.4.0

New things since last version:

Wolves now have nameplates with "Doggy" by default. Names can be changed with the first line of a placed sign, and pushing the wolf into the sign and right-clicking them with another sign.

Rather than specific wolf variants, they can now be trained to have the skills of the old variants, and can now have multiple skills (talents). Each new talent that is attempted to be trained has a lower chance, and becomes increasingly more difficult.

Training talents no longer uses the specific item, but now uses up bones. Still requires being right clicked by the specific item though.

Specific wolf variants no longer have a certain skin, and skins can be changed with a Lapis Lazuli Block. By default, the skins no longer have a ring around the tail, and just the collar is colored.

Mobs in this Version:

Wolves

Wolves are re-name-able, and are all named "Doggy" by default. Their names can be changed with 2 sign items. Place the sign and type your desired name on the first line only, then push your Doggy into the sign and right click with the second sign item.

Wolves that reach 0 HP do not permanently die, rather loose their perks and become weak. All can be recovered by feeding them a Cake.

Wolves can be released back into the wild using Shears. It is a 1/64 chance to work, but takes no durability to do so. They keep their old talents.

Tamed Wolves can be upgraded by teaching them new talents. There are several levels for a specific talent, and the chance of a successful upgrade is lowered each time it gains a new level.

Skills for Wolves

BlackPelt

A Wolf skill using Leather. Increases the Wolf's attack power on successful level up.

GuardDog

A Wolf skill using an Iron Ingot. Increases the Wolf's defense on successful level up.

HellHound

A Wolf skill using Netherrack. Increases the Wolf's immunity to Fire and Lava on successful level up.

PillowPaw

A Wolf skill using a Feather. Increases the Wolf's immunity to fall damage on successful level up.

Creeper Sweeper

A Wolf skill using Gunpowder. Wolves will growl at Creepers to warn you.

DogLight

A Wolf skill using a Glowstone Block. Wolves will produce more Torches when overfed per level.
