Doggy Talents v0.4.8

Mobs in this Version:

Wolves

Wolves are re-name-able, and are all named "Doggy" by default. Their names can be changed with 2 sign items. Place the sign and type your desired name on the first line only, then push your Doggy into the sign and right click with the second sign item.

Wolves that reach 0 HP do not permanently die, rather loose their perks and become weak. All can be recovered by feeding them a Cake.

Wolves can be released back into the wild using Shears while at full health. It is a 1/64 chance to work, but takes no durability to do so. They keep their old talents.

Tamed Wolves can be upgraded by teaching them new talents. There are several levels for a specific talent, and the chance of a successful upgrade is lowered each time it gains a new level. Upgrading uses an available Bone in the inventory.

You can view a Wolf's talent mastery by right-clicking on it with a Stick in hand.

Skills for Wolves

BlackPelt

A Wolf skill using Leather. Increases the Wolf's attack power on successful level up.

GuardDog

A Wolf skill using an Iron Ingot. Increases the Wolf's defense on successful level up.

HellHound

A Wolf skill using Netherrack. Increases the Wolf's immunity to Fire and Lava on successful level up.

PillowPaw

A Wolf skill using a Feather. Increases the Wolf's immunity to fall damage on successful level up.

Creeper Sweeper

A Wolf skill using Gunpowder. Wolves will growl at Creepers to warn you.

DogLight

A Wolf skill using a Glowstone Block. Wolves will produce more Torches when overfed per level.

WolfMount

A Wolf skill using a Saddle. Wolves are ride-able, and run faster per level. Can be mounted by right-clicking the Wolf while in the air.
