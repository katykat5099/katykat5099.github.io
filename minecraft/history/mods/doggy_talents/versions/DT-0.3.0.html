Doggy Talents v0.3.0

Mobs in this Version:

Wolves

Wolves that reach 0 HP do not permanently die, rather loose their perks and become weak. They can be revived with a Cake.

Wolves can be released back into the wild using Shears. It is a 1/64 chance to work, but takes no durability to do so. They keep their old talents.

Tamed Wolves can be upgraded into 6 different upgraded variants, using 6 different items. The chance is about 1/16 to succeed.

Fighter Wolf

A tamed wolf upgrade using Leather. Does about double the damage of a normal tamed wolf.

Creeper Sweeper

A tamed wolf upgrade using Gunpowder. Reacts to nearby creepers.

GlownWolf

A tamed wolf upgrade using Glowstone. Drops 4 torches when fed at full health.

Pillow Paw

A tamed wolf upgrade using Feathers. Immune to fall damage.

Sea Dog

A tamed wolf upgrade using Snow Blocks. Immune to drowning.

Hell Hound

A tamed wolf upgrade using Netherrack. Immune to fire.

Other Information:

BUG: The Sea Dog reverts back into a vanilla tamed wolf when the world is reloaded.
