MineCrap

What the heck is MineCrap? Is that a typo?

You read that correctly. This is indeed called MineCrap. It's a new Minecraft Era Project using a series of Modpacks called "MineCrap".

What's the gimmick? Like ALPHA WORLD, the world goes through a series of Minecraft updates. However, this time there are Mods added.

There's 5 versions that are currently planned to go through.
