Buckleo

An ancient spirit that sticks around for some strange reason. It is one of 3 spirits, this one giving guidance in defensive abilities.

Buckleo is pretty aggressive. It will attack you before you notice it to gain an advantage, but will keep a guard up otherwise.

Due to similarities, there is reasonable suspection that Buckleo has some common ancestral lineage to Lauleo.
