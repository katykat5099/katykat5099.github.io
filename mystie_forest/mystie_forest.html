Inspired by the history of the First Nations people of America, a mixture of different regional cultures with a Pokemon (and personal) twist.

You set out to learn how the strange forest, Mystie Forest, behaves. Why is it so hostile? How can this beast of nature be tamed, if at all?

Just as it seems all things are going perfectly for you, progress takes a massive turn as you stumble upon something more surprising than the forest itself.
It is then when you realize, things are only just beginning.

Most things are a WIP.
